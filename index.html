Explore the Pokemon World

Journey through all the regions of the Pokemon universe! From Kanto to Paldea, discover the unique landscapes, cities, and legendary Pokemon that make each region special.

Kanto

The first region in the Pokemon world. Home to the original 151 Pokemon and iconic cities like Pallet Town, Cerulean City, and the Pokemon League.

Johto

A region rich in tradition and history. Features the legendary Ho-Oh and Lugia, the Ecruteak Dance Theater, and the iconic Burned Tower.

Hoenn

An island region known for its diverse environments. Home to the ancient Pokemon Kyogre, Groudon, and Rayquaza who shaped the land, sea, and sky.

Sinnoh

A region of myths and legends. Features Mt. Coronet at its center and is home to Dialga, Palkia, and the creator Pokemon Arceus.

Unova

A modern region inspired by New York. Home to Reshiram and Zekrom, representing ideals and truth in an urban landscape.

Kalos

A beautiful region inspired by France. Introduced Fairy-type Pokemon and the legendary life Pokemon Xerneas and destruction Pokemon Yveltal.

Alola

A tropical island paradise inspired by Hawaii. Features Island Trials, Guardian Deities, and the legendary Solgaleo and Lunala.

Galar

A region inspired by Great Britain. Introduced Dynamax phenomenon and the legendary hero Pokemon Zacian and Zamazenta.

Hisui

Ancient Sinnoh in a bygone era. Experience the origins of the Pokedex and encounter Pokemon in the wild like never before.

Paldea

An open-world region inspired by the Iberian Peninsula. Features Terastal phenomenon and the legendary Koraidon and Miraidon.
