Discover the Most Powerful Pokemon

Welcome to the ultimate guide for Legendary Pokemon! These rare and powerful creatures are the stuff of myths and legends, each possessing incredible abilities and playing crucial roles in the Pokemon world.

Legendary Birds

The original trio from Kanto: Articuno (Ice/Flying), Zapdos (Electric/Flying), and Moltres (Fire/Flying). Masters of the skies.

Legendary Beasts

The Johto trio: Raikou (Electric), Entei (Fire), and Suicune (Water). Born from the ashes and blessed by Ho-Oh.

Weather Trio

The ancient super-ancient Pokemon of Hoenn: Kyogre (Water), Groudon (Ground), and Rayquaza (Dragon/Flying). Shapers of land, sea, and sky.

Creation Trio

Sinnoh's legendary dragons: Dialga (Time), Palkia (Space), and Giratina (Antimatter). Created by Arceus to shape reality itself.
